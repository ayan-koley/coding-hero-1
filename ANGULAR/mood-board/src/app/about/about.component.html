Welcome to Your Mood Tracker

Track your mood every day and see how your emotions change throughout the month.

Purpose of This Web App

This platform is designed to help you track your moods day by day. Over time, you'll be able to reflect on how your mood shifts and find patterns in your emotions. This can help you improve self-awareness and emotional health.
